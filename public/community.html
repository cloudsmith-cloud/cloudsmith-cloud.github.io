Who's building this

CloudSmith is being built by Hybrid Cloud Solutions (hybridsolutions.cloud) — Azure Local and Hyper-V specialists based in the US. We've been living in these environments for years, and CloudSmith is the tool we always wanted but couldn't find.

The ethos

We're building this for the community, not as a proprietary product with a cloud-only escape hatch. If you're tired of managing Azure Local with a mix of scripts, spreadsheets, and manual processes — this is for you. Open source. Self-hostable. Extensible. No vendor lock-in. No forced cloud dependency. Your infrastructure, your control.
